Usman Mazhar

Visiting Assistant Professor · Department of Geography, University of Gujrat

Geographer and climate scientist working at the intersection of remote sensing, GeoAI, and atmospheric science. My research integrates satellite observations, reanalysis datasets, and machine learning to investigate surface energy balance, aerosol radiative forcing, and land use dynamics under changing climate conditions.

Research Interests

01GeoAI and machine learning for integrating remote sensing, reanalysis, and ground-based datasets in climate change monitoring.
02Radiative forcing effects of aerosols and greenhouse/trace gases on atmospheric parameters and climate systems.
03Deep learning for land cover/land use change analysis, urban heat islands, and surface temperature dynamics.
04Data-driven approaches to surface energy balance, atmospheric processes, and ecosystem interactions under climate change.
